Ingredients
 - 4 cups water
 - 3 to 4 cups maize meal

Directions:
 1. Bring water to a boil in a pot(sufuria).

 2. Add maize meal and stir to prevent lumps.

 3. Add more maize meal to make a thick mush.

 4. Keep stirring until the maize meal is well cooked.

 5. Tip out onto a plate-the ugali should hold its shape.
 
 6. Best served hot with meat/fish.
